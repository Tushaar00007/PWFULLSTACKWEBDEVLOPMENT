Web Design

Web design is the process of planning, designing, and creating websites. It is a broad term that encompasses many different aspects of website development, including:
User experience (UX) design: This is the process of designing websites that are easy to use and enjoyable for users.
User interface (UI) design: This is the process of designing the look and feel of a website, including the layout, typography, colors, and images.
Web development: This is the process of building the website, including the code, content, and functionality.
Web marketing: This is the process of promoting and marketing a website to attract visitors.
