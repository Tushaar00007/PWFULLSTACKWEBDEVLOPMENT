Q-What are HTML Entities/ list out 5 commonly used HTML entities.

ans-HTML entities are special characters that are used to represent characters that cannot be typed directly on a keyboard. They are used in HTML to display special characters, such as copyright symbols, trademarks, and currency symbols.

The; commonly used HTMLentities are
&lt; - Less than sign
&gt; - Greater than sign
&amp; - Ampersand
&quot; - Double quotation mark
&copy; - Copyright symbol
