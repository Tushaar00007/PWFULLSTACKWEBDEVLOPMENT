1. strong - Used as emphasize text.
    • Use the strong tag to emphasize important text, such as headings, titles, and keywords.
    • Do not overuse the strong tag, as it can make your text difficult to read.
  2. em - Used to italicize text.
    • Use the em tag to italicize text that is important, but not as important as text that is emphasized with the strong tag.
    • Do not overuse the em tag, as it can make your text difficult to read.
  3. i - makes the text italics
    • The i tag is the same as the em tag, but it is deprecated in HTML5.
    • You should use the em tag instead of the i tag.
  4. b - makes the text bold.
    • Use the b tag to make text bold that is important, but not as important as text that is emphasized with the strong tag.
    • Do not overuse the b tag, as it can make your text difficult to read.
  5. u - underlines the text.
    • The u tag is deprecated in HTML5.
    • You should not use the u tag.
  6. s - strike through text.
    • The s tag is deprecated in HTML5.
    • You should not use the s tag.
